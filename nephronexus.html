Public Data in Nephrology: A Researcher’s Roadmap

This page is designed to support nephrology researchers by guiding you to a variety of publicly available data sources, from patient-level registries to multi-omic datasets. Here, you’ll find a curated list of key datasets.

The accompanying manuscript provides practical advice on accessing and using these resources to enhance your research. Whether you’re validating a hypothesis, exploring new analytical methods, or developing collaborative projects, we hope these data can advance your work without the high costs associated with data generation. Embrace the opportunity to innovate, discover, and contribute to kidney research in a way that’s efficient, equitable, and accessible to all.

This resource will be continually updated but please contact us if you have suggestions for new or missing resources.
